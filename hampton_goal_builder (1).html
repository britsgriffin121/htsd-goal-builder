5
Evidence of Impact

Tell us where this goal lives in your team's journey — we'll recommend the right types of evidence to collect.

T1
Participation & Reach
Who showed up, how many, how often
Start here ▼
Leading indicators that your work is actually happening. Best for new initiatives where you're building the foundation — measuring engagement, attendance, and who's involved before worrying about outcomes.
T2
Perception & Learning
Did knowledge, attitudes, or confidence shift?
Start here ▼
Process indicators that capture how people are experiencing the work. Surveys, reflections, and pre/post check-ins reveal whether mindsets and understanding are shifting — a key signal before you can expect practice to change.
T3
Practice & Implementation
Is the work showing up in classrooms and buildings?
Prioritize this ▼
Fidelity indicators that show whether learning is translating into real instructional shifts. Artifacts, learning walks, and portfolio samples demonstrate that the work has moved from intention to practice.
T4
Impact on Learners
What changed for students?
Set impact goals ▼
Outcome indicators that show what's changed for students — growth in competencies, quality of work, long-term dispositions. These take time to emerge and require a solid foundation in Tiers 1–3 first.
